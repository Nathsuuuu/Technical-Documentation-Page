Introduction

Basketball is a sport played by two teams of five players on a rectangular court. The objective is to shoot a ball through a hoop 18 inches (46 cm) in diameter and mounted at a height of 10 feet (3.048 m) to backboards at each end of the court.

The NBA (National Basketball Association) is a professional basketball league in North America composed of 30 teams. It is one of the major professional sports leagues in the United States and Canada and the premier men's professional basketball league in the world.

Michael Jordan

Michael Jordan is widely regarded as the greatest basketball player of all time. He won six NBA championships with the Chicago Bulls and earned five MVP awards.

  • 6-time NBA Champion
  • 5-time MVP
  • 14-time All-Star
  • 10-time scoring champion
  • Hall of Fame Inductee
